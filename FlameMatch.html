About FLAMES

FLAMES is a popular relationship prediction game that originated in schoolyards across Asia in the 1990s.
The game uses a mathematical elimination process based on the unique letters in two names.
Each letter in FLAMES represents a different relationship outcome - from Friends to Marriage!
